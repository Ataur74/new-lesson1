Welcome to Bangladeshi Blog

We live in Bangladesh. She is an independent country. She got freedom in 1971 from Pakistan. We speak in Bangla. People of different religions,professions,etc.live in our country. We like things available here. So, I don't go to seek happiness in others' lands.

Bangladeshi Foods

We eat rice as our main food in Bangladesh. We eat it three times a day. We eat breakfast, lunch and dinner with rice, fish, vegetables and pulse.We eat different types of fruits and sweets. We prepare borhani, a very tasty drink. Biriyani is a typical festival dish. We like local foods available here. So, I don't import foods.

We are hospitable

We live in extended family in Bangladesh. We are social and friendly. We help one another in our problems. None is alone. We are cooperative and helpful. So, sometimes we reach you at your personal life and matters even. Feel free to ask evrything in details.
